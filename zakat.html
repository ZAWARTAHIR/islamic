Zakat Guide

Basic rules and a simple calculator for Zakat.

What is Zakat?

Zakat is an obligatory alms-giving on eligible wealth. This page will explain nisab, calculation and examples.

Calculator (coming soon)

We will add an interactive calculator to compute Zakat based on assets and liabilities.

Nisab Explained

Nisab is the minimum amount of wealth one must have before Zakat becomes due; commonly measured against gold or silver values.

What Counts

Cash, savings, business inventory, and certain investments are commonly included; personal items and primary residence are usually excluded.

Example

Calculate annual Zakat as 2.5% of eligible wealth above the nisab after one lunar year.
