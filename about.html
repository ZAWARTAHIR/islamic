About Ramzan Calendar

A simple, modern tool to view Sehri and Iftar timings across cities.

Purpose

This project provides Ramadan timings and quick resources for users worldwide.

Team

A small team of volunteers and developers maintain this site to provide accurate and friendly information.

Data Sources

Timings are sourced from public timetables, astronomical calculations, and contributions from local mosques.

Open Source

We welcome contributions and corrections; consider sharing local timings or fixes via the contact page.
