About Ramzan Calendar

A simple, modern tool to view Sehri and Iftar timings across cities.

Purpose

This project provides Ramadan timings and quick resources for users worldwide.

Home

The Home page is the starting point for users. It shows today's date, a short summary of upcoming sehri/iftar times, quick links to the most-used pages, and highlights such as announcements or featured cities.

Use this page to get an immediate overview without selecting a city; links guide you to deeper tools like the calendar and timings.

Calendar

The Calendar page provides a month-by-month view for Ramadan. Each day displays estimated Sehri and Iftar times. Users can navigate between months and click a day for details.

This view is useful for planning ahead, sharing dates, and quickly scanning the whole month at a glance.

Timings

The Timings page lets users select their country and city to see precise prayer and fasting times for that location. It combines astronomical calculations with available local data.

Features typically include: timezone-aware times, an option to switch jurisprudence/method where supported, and daily updates or corrections.

Duas

The Duas page contains commonly recited supplications for Sehri, Iftar, Laylat al-Qadr, general needs, and other occasions. Each dua shows the original Arabic and an English translation.

We keep these concise for quick reading during daily worship. Let us know if you want additional duas or transliterations added.

Zakat

The Zakat page explains the basics of Zakat—who must pay, what assets are considered, and common nisab thresholds. When available, a simple calculator helps estimate a user's annual obligation.

Content here is educational and should not replace local religious advice; local rules may vary by jurisdiction and school of thought.

Lunar

The Lunar page focuses on moon-phase information and the lunar calendar. It includes visual indicators of the moon phase and optional theme controls that adjust the site's visual elements (moon, stars, sun) according to the selected theme.

This page is useful for users who want to track lunar phases or understand how the calendar aligns with astronomical cycles.

About

This About page provides an overview of the project, its goals, the data sources we rely on, and how contributors can get involved. It also contains this site guide and pointers to the repository and contact methods.

Contact

The Contact page allows users to send feedback, report issues, or submit corrections. The page includes a contact form and a WhatsApp quick-message option that opens the user's WhatsApp with a prefilled message to the configured number.

For corrections to timings, please include the city and source so we can verify and apply updates promptly.

Team

A small team of volunteers and developers maintain this site to provide accurate and friendly information.

Data Sources

Timings are sourced from public timetables, astronomical calculations, and contributions from local mosques.

Open Source

We welcome contributions and corrections; consider sharing local timings or fixes via the contact page.
