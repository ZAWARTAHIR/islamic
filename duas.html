Duas for Ramzan

Short duas used during Sehri, Iftar and special nights.

Sehri Intention

وَبِصَوْمِ غَدٍ نَّوَيْتُ مِنْ شَهْرِ رَمَضَانَ

I intend to fast tomorrow in the month of Ramzan.

Iftar Dua

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي لَكَ صُمْتُ وَبِكَ آمَنْتُ وَعَلَيْكَ تَوَكَّلْتُ

O Allah, I fasted for You, and I believe in You and I put my trust in You.

Short Bismillah

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ

In the name of Allah — commonly recited before breaking the fast.

Dua for Forgiveness

رَبِّ اغْفِرْ لِي

My Lord, forgive me.

Laylat al-Qadr Dua

اللهم إنك عفو تحب العفو فاعف عني

O Allah, You are Most Forgiving and love forgiveness, so forgive me.

Dua for Parents

رَبِّ اغْفِرْ لِي وَلِوَالِدَيَّ

My Lord, forgive me and my parents.

Protection Dua

أَعُوذُ بِكَلِمَاتِ اللَّهِ التَّامَّاتِ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ

I seek refuge in the perfect words of Allah from the evil of what He created.

Dua for Guidance

اهْدِنَا الصِّرَاطَ الْمُسْتَقِيمَ

Guide us to the straight path.

Acceptance Dua

اللهم تَقَبَّلْ مِنَّا

O Allah, accept from us (our worship and deeds).

General Supplication

اللهم اغننا بحلالك عن حرامك وبفضلك عمن سواك

O Allah, suffice us with what You have allowed instead of what You have forbidden, and with Your bounty instead of needing others.
